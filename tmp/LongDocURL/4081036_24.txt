 
Our gross profit margin increased from 36% to 56% for the three months ended April 30, 2021 as compared to the three months ended April 30, 2020 primarily due to the
lower cost of product and service revenue. Gross product profit margin increased from 49% to 75% for the three months ended April 30, 2021 as compared to the three
months ended April 30, 2020 primarily due to a reduction in third-party hardware costs and an increase in higher margin software sales. Gross service profit margins
increased from 26% to 47% for the three months ended April 30, 2021 as compared to the three months ended April 30, 2020 primarily due to a reduction in headcount in
relation to our cost-saving efforts driven by the COVID-19 pandemic which drove higher margins.
